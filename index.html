About ScopeIN Studio

ScopeIN Studio is an independent game development studio founded by Kanishk Sharma and Sakshi Sharma. We are dedicated to creating high-quality, anime-inspired games with immersive storytelling, breathtaking visuals, and innovative gameplay. Our goal is to build experiences that players will remember for years to come.
